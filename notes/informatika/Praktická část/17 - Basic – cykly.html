17 - Basic – cykly

FOR – známý počet opakování

DIM i AS INTEGER
FOR i = 1 TO 10
    PRINT i
NEXT i

S krokem:

FOR i = 0 TO 100 STEP 10
    PRINT i           ' 0, 10, 20, ..., 100
NEXT i

WHILE – dokud platí podmínka

DIM i AS INTEGER
i = 1
WHILE i <= 10
    PRINT i
    i = i + 1
WEND

DO…LOOP UNTIL – dokud neplatí

DIM j AS INTEGER
j = 1
DO
    PRINT j
    j = j + 1
LOOP UNTIL j > 10

Klasický příklad: součet 1 až N

DIM n AS INTEGER, i AS INTEGER, soucet AS INTEGER
INPUT "Zadej n: ", n
soucet = 0
FOR i = 1 TO n
    soucet = soucet + i
NEXT i
PRINT "Součet 1 až "; n; " je "; soucet
